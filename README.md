# 🚗 Car Rental Sales & Profit Analysis – Power BI Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a **Power BI dashboard analyzing car rental business performance** using booking data.
The goal is to transform raw rental transaction data into **meaningful business insights** that help understand revenue generation, profitability, booking behavior, and customer trends.

The dashboard provides a **comprehensive view of sales, profit, rental patterns, and customer behavior** across different car types, fuel types, booking channels, and months.

📌 Business Problem
Car rental companies manage large volumes of booking data across different vehicle types, fuel categories, booking channels, and cities. Without proper   analysis, it becomes difficult to identify revenue drivers, customer booking behavior, and operational inefficiencies.
The business needs a data-driven solution to monitor sales performance, profitability, booking trends, and customer patterns to support better decision-making.  
        
🎯 Goal of the Dashboard
The main goal of this dashboard is to transform raw booking data into actionable business insights that help the company:
Monitor overall revenue and profitability
Track booking trends over time
Identify high-performing car types and fuel categories
Understand customer booking behavior
Analyze online vs offline booking channels
Support data-driven operational and business decisions

KPI Metrics
The dashboard begins with key performance indicators that summarize business performance:
    Total Bookings: 561
    Total Sales: ₹16,408,480
    Total Profit: ₹5,712,005
    Average Rental Days: 10
    Accepted Bookings: 447
    Accepted Rate: 79.68%
    Profit Margin: 34.81%
    
💡 Business Impact & Insights
    From the dashboard analysis, several important insights were discovered:
        The company generated ₹16.4M revenue and ₹5.7M profit, indicating strong overall business performance.
        Luxury vehicles generate the highest revenue and profit, making them a key driver of business growth.
        Diesel vehicles provide the highest profitability among fuel types.
        Online bookings dominate the rental channel, showing strong adoption of digital platforms.
        Peak rental demand occurs in August and December, likely due to travel seasons.
        Around 20% of bookings are rejected, which may indicate fleet availability limitations or operational constraints.
        The customer base consists of 57% new customers and 43% returning customers, suggesting opportunities to improve customer retention. 

        
Business takeaways:
    Increasing Luxury vehicle inventory could increase revenue. 
    Diesel fleet still provides strong financial return.
    Increase car availability during peak months.
    The company should invest more in online marketing and booking platforms.
    Improving fleet size or scheduling could increase accepted bookings.
    Weekend demand is lower than expected.Company could run weekend promotions.
